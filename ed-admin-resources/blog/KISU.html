Kampala International School transformed their school management experience with Ed-admin

The story of Kampala International School Uganda

KISU is a warm, vibrant and truly international school located in a very beautiful and friendly country. They believe passionately in a holistic and values-based approach to education that seeks to develop the person as well as the learner.

The opportunity of managing everything from one place

KISU :

Previously, our administration system had been ad hoc and produced unnecessary paperwork.

We had student records in various places; for example, the Library used a system called Oliver, and admissions used a simple spreadsheet in conjunction with paper files. The sports department was in charge of ECA, and each sports director had their own system. We also used a different reporting system depending on the secondary leader. As a result, it was difficult to find information when one was looking for it because there was no system that allowed all of the information to be in one place.

Why Kampala International School Chose Ed-admin

The Results

For starters, Ed-admin has enabled us to organise the school and run everything from a single location. We can provide live statistics and accurate information about students and other items whenever required. Furthermore, Ed-admin has enabled us to keep our data safe for an extended period of time, allowing us as an institution to access data dating back to when we first implemented it in the school 12 years ago.
Another thing we've been able to do is planning our way up the ladder in the future.
