The best online report card system every school needs

Ed-admin education management software is a time-saver, especially in the report card season.

Assessment Premium module allows teachers to keep attendance and results of various assignments, tests, and quizzes of each student throughout the year on a cloud-based system. Gone is the time when teachers had to enter the results of each student manually and calculate the overall grade. Now, Ed-admin does all the work for you. It keeps the results and grades, adds them up, and generate the report card of your choice. Cloud-based

A cloud-based system guarantees that your information is secure, reliable, and accurate. Under any circumstances, you cannot lose a piece of information. Everything is saved on the system automatically, and you can have access to them from any device and any browser as long as you have your login info. In addition to keeping the information safe and secure, the results saved in the grade book are absolutely accurate and proof against tech glitches. Data moves through Ed-admin Assessment Module. Grades saved in the grade book and assessment module, flow later to the report cards, making them as reliable as possible.

Custom Report Cards

The great thing about the Ed-admin report card is that it is highly customisable. Schools can design their own report card according to different grades, classes, teachers’ preferences, students’ behaviour, and basically any specific need. Ed-admin makes sure that the report card is exactly what you designed, and contains the information you required. Transcripts

Transcripts contain all the grades of one student’s school journey. Mostly, transcripts are used for high school students who seek further education and their complete academic performance is needed. With Ed-admin transcripts, you can monitor and track students' grades in charts or graphs.

Report Cards contain the grades of a particular period of education. Since Ed-admin keeps all the information throughout the year in a cloud, teachers never have to enter the grades to get a report card. By choosing the grade, the period of time, and the template, report cards are easily generated with the Ed-admin Assessment Premium Module.
