Health Screening via NFC/QR/Barcode Supported ID Card Scan - Now Available in the App Store

We wanted to support staff in recording health statistics for their staff and students, in line with many government guidelines around the world. That’s why we developed a version of the Edana Staff App that includes the “Health Screening” feature.

The newest update makes the screening process even faster by integrating NFC supported ID cards. The app can also scan the Student/Staff ID cards (in the form of Barcode or QR code).

That’s why we at Ed-admin have accelerated the development of a brand new feature called “Health Screening” in the “Edana Staff Portal App”. This module will allow institutions to record vital data about student health and safety quickly and easily. The application has a symptom checker, including information on symptom severity, so that users can make informed decisions to keep their student safe.

Download the latest Android & iOS App from Google Play and iOS App store to use these features. The app versions are:
- Android App version 2.4
- iOS App version 2.3
