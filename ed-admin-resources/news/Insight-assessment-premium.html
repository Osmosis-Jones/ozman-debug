Insight Tab: Assessment Premium

We're delighted to announce the addition of a new section to the Assessment Premium Module. The new Insights tab is intended to assist educators in better understanding, identifying, and responding to the needs of individual students. You may now see a quick view of students' attendance, birthdays, health data, registration and leaving dates, Learning Support records, Parent and Teacher Notes, Learning and Behaviour records, and educators' comments in different subjects altogether under the Insights tab.
