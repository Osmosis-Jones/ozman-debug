Save time managing Moodle using Ed-admin’s new integration

We are excited to announce that Ed-admin is now fully integrated with Moodle, one of the most popular learning management systems in the world. Moodle users can now synchronize their data with Ed-admin with just a few clicks.

Moodle integration saves you time on:

  • Account creation: create Moodle accounts from Ed-admin, no need to create accounts across two systems
  • Subjects and class setup: create automatically in Moodle from subjects and classes in the Ed-admin timetable
  • Assessment grades: automatic synchronization from Moodle to the Ed-admin grade book (in the Assessment Premium module)

To take advantage of this time-saving add-on, please log into the client portal and find Moodle Integration under the Development tab. Click ‘Proceed’ and it’s done.
