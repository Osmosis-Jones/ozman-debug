Edana Student app: A pocket full of potential

There is no escape from technology. Any child born since the millennium has had the internet as their constant companion. As technology has become mainstream it has been assimilated into the learning environment. Many schools use computers and tablets in the classroom and give homework that requires the use of education apps. The use of technology in K-12 education is gaining remarkable popularity. Let’s take a closer look by studying the infographic below.

Edana Student Portal app is a free and accessible tool for all Ed-admin clients. It enriches students’ scholastic experience and saves administrators considerable time.

Edana Student app gives students everything they need to manage their education. They can attend online classes, view class schedules, and submit assignments, all from their phones.

Watch the short video below to learn more about the features.

Edana Student Portal app is already synced with your Ed-admin modules and no extra setup is required. Students need to only download the app on their devices and start using it immediately.

At this moment, you must be thinking that promoting an app to hundreds of students must be an extremely difficult and time-consuming undertaking. Rest assured, we have a solution for that too!

Using Ed-admin’s Communication module, and with only a few clicks, you can send a bulk email to all of your students with links to app stores, their activation code, an introduction of the app, and even a few screenshots to give them an idea of what to expect.
