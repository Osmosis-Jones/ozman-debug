New Features in Edana Parent Portal App

The Edana Parent Portal mobile app is an integral part of Ed-admin. The app has already made communication between guardians and institutions easier and more effective than ever before. Even so, at Ed-admin, we are committed to providing app users with a seamless experience and easy to use world-class features. That’s why we continue to release new features and improvements on our mobile apps.

The newest app update is now available with the following features:
- Version 2.3 for Android
- Version 2.2 for iOS

Update Emergency Contacts: Now it is easy for guardians to view or update the emergency contacts.

ECA Signup: Extracurricular activities play an essential role in a child’s mental and physical development. The new update allows guardians to register their child for sports and afterschool activities through the “Activities” module on the app.

Unread Notifications: The bell icon on top of the screen now displays the unread notification count, so that users don’t miss any vital information. Rich-Text Notifications: Unleashing the power of the hyperlinks in notifications, institutions can now post links and images in “Notification Details".
