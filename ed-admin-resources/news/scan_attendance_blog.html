Fast attendance taking: Scan student cards with your phone

Using the latest Edana Staff app update, you can now take attendance by scanning student cards using only your phone.

On your Edana Staff app, tap on My Learning Space and choose the appropriate term. You can record attendance for Classes, Subjects, Activities and House/Tutor Groups. After choosing your category and tapping on the Attendance header, you would be able to see the Scan icon on the top.

Tap the Scan button and choose the method of data collection among Barcode, QR code, and NFC.

Just as fast, you can start scanning student ID cards and enjoy automatic attendance taking.
