Ecentric

Make payments easy, safe, and fast using Ecentric payment gateway.

Features and Benefits:

  • Students paying tuition fees or other charges online using the student portal
  • Potential students paying for the application fee online while applying for admission
  • Parents or sponsors paying for their students fees and other charges using the Parent Portal or Edana Parent mobile app
  • Students or parents ordering products and paying for them online such as uniforms, Text books, etc
  • Payment gateways can be automated and fee receipts for all transactions are generated instantly and sent to parents via SMS, email, or mobile app alerts
  • Online payment gateways are open 24/7 and available to all internet-enabled devices
  • Payments can be made by credit or debit, in full or partially

If you like to activate the integration, use Client Portal to raise a ticket.
